Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new report?

After logging in, click "Start New Report" on your dashboard. Fill in all the required sections and click "Generate PDF Report" when complete.

Can I save my work and continue later?

Yes! Each section has a "Save Draft" button. You can save your progress at any time and continue editing later from your dashboard.

How do I save my digital signature?

When uploading a signature, check the "Save this signature for future use" checkbox. Your signature will be saved and you can select it from the dropdown in future reports.

What file formats are supported?

Images: JPG, PNG, GIF. Documents: PDF (will be converted to images, one page per image). Maximum file size: 10MB per file (automatically compressed if larger).

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

Can I upload large images?

Yes! The system automatically compresses and resizes large images to ensure PDF generation works smoothly. Images larger than 1200px or 10MB will be automatically optimized.

How are PDF documents handled?

PDF files are automatically converted to images, with each page becoming a separate image. This ensures all content is properly embedded in the final report.
